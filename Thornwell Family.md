# The Thornwell Legacy

**Magical Specialty:** Divination, Prophecy, and Foresight

- Ancient family known for advising nobility through scrying, prophecy, and fortune-telling
- Less politically powerful than the Starweaves, but sought after for their unique gifts
- Strategic marriage: Aldric saw value in binding a family of seers to the Starweaves

> "The Thornwells see what others cannot"

## Vivienne's Gift

- Genuinely talented at divination (more so than Cornelius at evocation/traditional wizardry)
- May have foreseen aspects of her children's futures, which complicates her feelings
- Perhaps saw Blastran's rebellion coming but couldn't/didn't prevent it
- This knowledge might make her sympathetic to him - she knows it's not "just a phase"
