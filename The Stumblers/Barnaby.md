## Character Overview
**Race/Class**: Tortle Artificer
**Background**: Traveling merchant/trader
**Player**: Sam

## Appearance & Personality
### Physical Description
Tortle with distinctive turtle shell-shaped wagon (damaged in ambush)
Carries potion-making supplies and various trade goods
### Personality Traits
Keeps his head down, cautious and observant
Bit of an upseller, entrepreneurial spirit
"Fake it til you make it" attitude
Cowardly in combat situations - retreats into shell when threatened

### Mannerisms & Speech
Business-focused, always looking for trading opportunities
Tends to retreat into his shell (literally) during danger
Calculating and practical in approach to problems
## Background & History
### Origins
Part of Tortle merchant heritage - his people used to be better traders with large caravans until airships made them obsolete
Family pressure to prove himself as successful merchant
Extensive knowledge of trade routes across the region
### Notable Life Events
Has been taken advantage of by [[Tabaxi]] traders in the past - now hates them
Established successful trade route network
Recruited by [[Luxor]] for specific ruby transport mission
## Notable Campaign Moments
### Key Events
**Trade Route Journey**: Travelled from [[Arastand]] through [[Farkeep]], [[Ironpine]], [[Hintermere]], [[Valeth]], [[Newguard]], [[Bleakvein]], back to [[Newguard]], then [[Oxborn]], arriving in [[Dawndale]]

**Ruby Supply Run**: Unknowingly transporting rubies for [[Luxor]] to [[Godmere]] - picked up [[Cnut]] at [[Arastand]], then travelled to [[Bleakvein]] to collect supply

**Orc Encounter**: Had a close call with orcs when traveling from [[Bleakvein]] to [[Newguard]]

**Session 1**: Retreated into his turtle shell wagon during the bandit ambush

**Session 2**: Successfully harvested 3x Lightning Moss from the town fountain

**Session 3**: Successfully created and sold hair tonic to [[Bjorn]] (with unexpected results)

**Session 4**: **CONTROVERSIAL ACTION** - Abandoned dying [[Cnut]], refused to heal him, leading to Cnut's death

### Character Development
Evolved from simple merchant to reluctant adventurer
Skills in alchemy and potion-making have proven valuable
**Major Crisis**: His abandonment of [[Cnut]] has fundamentally damaged party trust and relationships
