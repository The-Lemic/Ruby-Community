# Bronn

## Character Overview
**Race/Class**: Likely Paladin (storm powers, serves [[Talos]])
**Background**: 30-year memory gap due to mind wipe
**Player**: Stanton

## Appearance & Personality
### Physical Description
Carries [[Necklace of the Shades]] and wooden figure of [[Talos]]
Shows signs of someone who has lived through trauma and violence
### Personality Traits
Destructive streak but fundamentally good-hearted
Haunted by lost memories and disturbing flashbacks
Protective of party members, especially [[Barnaby]]
Skilled at practical survival tasks
### Mannerisms & Speech
Experiences violent flashbacks triggered by certain locations
Taps into dangerous storm powers connected to [[Talos]]
Pragmatic and direct in approach to problems
## Background & History
### Origins
Unknown due to 30-year memory wipe
Serves [[Talos]], the storm god
Previous involvement in systematic [[Everflame]] persecution
Many before him have been driven mad by [[Talos]]'s power
### Notable Life Events
**The Mind Wipe**: Lost 30 years of memory after mysterious mind wipe - unknown who performed it or why

`~{**[[Everflame]] Killing**: Killed an old miner 20 years ago because the miner's daughter was an [[Everflame]] - suggests involvement in systematic persecution}`

`~{**Service to [[Talos]]**: Ally to the storm god, tapping into dangerous powers that have driven others mad}`

## Notable Campaign Moments
### Key Events
**Session 1**: Moved to protect [[Barnaby]] during the bandit ambush, showing protective instincts

**Session 2**: Experienced disturbing flashbacks while in [[Dawnvale]] - visions of figures in blue cloaks going door-to-door (likely memories of [[Everflame]] purges)

**Session 3**: Expertly skinned a lion for [[Finric]], later harvested materials from the dead lion, demonstrating survival skills

**Session 4**: Heroically deflected lightning damage to protect [[Blastran]] and [[Marvin the Owlbear]], went down fighting alongside them

Session 5: Gained the javelin of lightning. Had a flashback at the [[Destroyed Kord's House]] where he saw [[Jason Kord]] and [[Emma Kord]] being attacked by people dressed in blue cloaks
### Character Development
Struggling to reconcile lost memories with present actions
Evolution from potential [[Everflame]] hunter to party protector
Grappling with the moral implications of his forgotten past
Knows deep down that he has done something awful
## Personal Quests & Goals
