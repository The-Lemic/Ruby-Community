# Strom

## Character Overview
**Race/Class**: Half-orc
**Background**: Son of the town protector
**Player**: Jamie

## Appearance & Personality
### Physical Description
Half-orc with mixed heritage features
Son of [[Zog Ironheart]] (orc barbarian)
### Personality Traits
Arrogant beyond his own ability
Wants to become his own man and live up to his father's legacy
History of drunken fights with townspeople
### Mannerisms & Speech
Can be combative and confrontational
Struggles with family expectations and disappointments
## Background & History
### Origins
Born in [[Dawnvale]] as son of [[Zog Ironheart]] (orc protector) and [[Gabb Ironheart]]
Only half-orc in the village, questions about his father's unique presence
Raised under pressure to live up to his father's reputation as protector
### Notable Life Events
Learned uncomfortable family truths - mother had affair with mad priest and ran off with sailor
Mother disappeared two weeks ago, secretly joined [[Order of the Old Gods]]
Family crisis has devastated both him and his father
## Notable Campaign Moments
### Key Events
**Session 1**: Attempted to fight back against [[Mad Nash]] but was quickly brought to his knees

**Session 2**: Learned uncomfortable family truths through town gossip about his mother's affair; [[Callie Tosslecobb]] was initially wary due to his previous drunken altercations

**Family Crisis**: Mother's disappearance revealed to be cult recruitment
### Character Development
Struggling with family legacy and personal identity
Learning to channel aggression constructively
Dealing with family trauma and mother's betrayal
